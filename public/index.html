Why It Matters

✨ Core Features

🎵 Verified Listening

Only completed listens (≥90%) are counted. Anti-bot, anti-seek, anti-spam filters protect the system.

💰 Split Payment

70% royalty to the artist, 30% incentive to the listener — automatically distributed on Arc.

🌐 Open Registry

Artists and listeners can self-register their wallets. No central authority required.
