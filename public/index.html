Automatically reward creators and users for genuine engagement — starting with music, expanding to all open-source content.

🎵 Join the Network

AttentionPool is not just a music royalty system. It's a Verified Attention Infrastructure — a protocol that verifies real engagement and automatically distributes micropayments. Music on Navidrome is the first implementation. Podcasts, audiobooks, and video are next.

⛓️ On-Chain Proof

Every verified listen triggers two Arc transactions: 70% creator royalty and 30% listener reward. All transactions are settled on Arc Testnet.

Why It Matters

🎯

Reward Attention, Not Clicks

Most streaming platforms reward clicks. AttentionPool rewards attention — a payment is only released after verified completion.

🛡️

Anti-Bot, Anti-Seek, Anti-Spam

Every play is validated: duration check, seek detection, daily limits, and cooldowns ensure genuine engagement.

💰

Fair Split: 70% / 30%

Artists earn 70% royalty. Listeners earn 30% incentive. Both benefit from verified attention.

💰 How Funding Works

🚀 Current Funding Model

AttentionPool Treasury currently funds rewards for verified listening. This treasury is used to demonstrate automated royalty distribution during the hackathon.

🎤 Artist-Funded Campaigns

Artists can fund their own reward pools to promote songs and reward verified listeners.

🎵 Playlist-Funded Campaigns

Playlist curators can allocate budgets to encourage discovery of independent music.

🤝 Sponsor-Funded Campaigns

Communities, labels, and sponsors can fund campaigns that distribute rewards based on verified attention.
